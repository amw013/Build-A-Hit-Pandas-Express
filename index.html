Our model says hit songs in this dataset tend to be:
  • More danceable and a bit louder (closer to 0 dB)
  • Less instrumental and slightly lower in valence
  • Tempo a bit above average, with energy not maxed out
  • Somewhat higher acousticness
Moving sliders in those directions usually increases the hit probability but only according to our simple model trained on this dataset. Real world hits still depend on hype, timing, and luck.
